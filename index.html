The History of the Earthquakes

Background

Earthquakes club were founded in June 15, 1994. It is a professional soccer team from the Western Conference of Major League Soccer. There stadium, PayPal Park, is located in San Jose, Ca. Their first title came in 2001. Former rival clubs are LA Galaxy and LAFC, both clubs are located in the same state as the Earthquakes.
